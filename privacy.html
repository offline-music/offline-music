Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 6, 2026

🔒 Our Privacy Commitment

Offline Music ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. We are committed to protecting your data and maintaining transparency. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our music player application.

1. Introduction

Offline Music is a music player application focused on offline playback, playlist management, and a smooth listening experience. By using Offline Music, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect and Process

Offline Music is designed to minimize data collection. Depending on how you use the app, we may process limited information such as:

  • Usage and Technical Data: We may receive crash diagnostics, analytics events, device model, operating system version, and app performance data through our service providers.
  • Locally Stored Preferences: Playback history, app settings, and similar preferences may be stored on your device.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information, payment card details, contacts, precise location data, microphone recordings, or camera recordings for core app operation.

3. User Content

Offline Music plays music files stored locally on your device. When you use the app:

  • Your music files remain on your device and are not uploaded to our servers.
  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • You are responsible for the content you choose to play through the application.

4. Third-Party Services & Data Collection

To improve our service, we may use third-party service providers that may process limited data:

  • App diagnostics and analytics services: Used to understand app stability, performance, and usage patterns.
  • Network service providers: Used only when optional online features are enabled.

These services may process information such as your device model, OS version, app usage statistics, and network request metadata in accordance with their own privacy policies.

5. Data Security

We take data security seriously. Offline Music processes most data locally on your device. Any network requests are transmitted via encrypted secure protocols (HTTPS/TLS) where applicable. Please note that while we strive to protect your information, no transmission over the internet can be guaranteed as 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Offline Music is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently processed such information, please contact us immediately.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights:

  • Stop Data Transmission: You can limit future data interaction by uninstalling the application.
  • Contact Us: For any privacy-related questions, you can reach out to us directly.
